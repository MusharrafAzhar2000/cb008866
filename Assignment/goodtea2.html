How to Pick Quality Tea

It doesn't matter if you are new to tea Culture or an experienced tea drinker who has been drinking tea for a long time. One must know how to find high-quality tea among these abundance of tea Manufacturers. It doesn't take Special Skill set to find high-quality tea, you can do it by simply using four of Your Senses.


Using your Sight

Tea processed using CTC Method

Tea are processed two ways. One is CTC Method, the Other is Orthodox Method. In the CTC Method. Tea leaves are cut, torn and curled into small bits using machinery. This method is used for tea which are then packed into tea bags. This method is very effiecient when tea is being processed at large quantities. This method gives dark Strong brew in a small interval But after the CTC method tea loses most of it's aroma.

In the Orthodox Method , Tea leaves are handled delicately to minimize the breakage. These tea leaves are not cut or torn. They are carefully rolled so the tea doesn't lose it's flavor or aroma.


Tea processed using Orthodox Method

So Tea which are processed using Orthodox method are considered high-quality tea. Try to avoid loose tea that are processed using CTC method. They are less flavorful and they might have lost most of their aroma.

By Touching

High Quality tea leaves are soft to touch but they will feel and look sturdy so you will feel the slight weight in hand. if the tea leaf feels light and feels dusty when you touch it maybe the tea is old. If the tea leaf breaks or crumbles easilyit may be because it is overdried. If your tea tends to get damaged even after gentle handling it maybe because the tea is not good quality.

By Using your sense of smell

Tea has a distinct aroma despite the type of tea.If your tea leaves has only a slight smell it maybe because it is low quality or old. Every tea type has a smell of it's own. White tea and black tea has a earthy floral smell, Green tea has a smell similar to freshly cut grass.

By Tasting


This Might not be a surprise but high quality tea will have good taste but, we will try to get to what kind of taste you should expect. Regardless of what type of tea you are drinking, Every tea will have an excellent taste if they are good quality. high-quality White and black tea has a rich, intense taste. While green tea and oolong tea has a refreshing smooth flavor.When Drinking tea try as much as you can to embrace the flavor. Don't gulp it down in one sip even if it's tempting.
